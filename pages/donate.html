Donation

We want your support by gathering your unused items from your home
and office which you will scrap not to ever use again.
This may be scrap for you but can be new for someone in need.

This donation will help to fulfill the basic needs of the under
privileged people and donations help sustain our organization.
This will also benefit the community which we
serve and together we can bring a smile to a lot of people's faces.

Since our inception, we have received donations from 1000+ donors.

What can be donated?

List of things which can be donated:

  • All standard college/school/technical education/old books
  • Novels & Story books
  • Blank notebooks, registers, bill books, etc.
  • Newspapers & Stationery
  • Used Laptops, Computers & any electronic parts
  • Used Sewing Machine & any used electronic appliances
  • Used water bottles, tiffin box, compass box,
    school bag/ office bag/ travel bag
  • Old clothes/ Uniforms
  • Any scrap
